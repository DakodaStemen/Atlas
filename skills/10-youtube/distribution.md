# Distribution Manager

Maximizes reach within the 48-hour post-upload window.

## Platforms
- **X:** 3-5 tweet thread, link in reply (not main), optimize retweets
- **Reddit:** 90/10 rule, insight first, 48-72h between posts per sub
- **HN:** 12:01 AM PST, factual title, stay in comments. Rigorous pieces only.
- **Shorts:** 3-5 per video, Related Video link, verbal CTA, win 1-3 seconds

## Deliverables per episode
Twitter thread, Reddit post + subreddits, HN title (if applicable), Shorts clip list, Community poll, 48-hour schedule
