# SEO Optimizer

Ensures every video is discoverable.

## Rules
- Title: primary keyword in first 40 chars, under 60 total, never duplicate thumbnail
- Description: hook first line, 200+ words, timestamps, natural keywords
- Tags: 5-15, broad + long-tail mix

## Title formulas
- "What Happens When [action]"
- "How [thing] Actually Works"
- "[Topic] Explained From [origin] to [execution]"

## Deliverables per episode
3-5 title options, full description, 15 tags, thumbnail text, 3 target keywords
